Multicast Chat Demo App

    Welcome to Multicast Chat Demo App!

  • This app allows you to send messages to you local network without help of any server.
  • [Show help]
    • Whoever installed this app in your local network can see the messages you send. They are listed on the right panel.
    • Press Enter to send message; Press Shift+Enter for new lines.
    • Double click on your name to edit it.
    • [Warning] Messages are transferred in public with plain text. Due to the nature of UDP protocol, your message could be lost randomly.
